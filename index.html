Explore Kelardasht

The lost paradise of northern Iran

Klardasht mountain region is one of the dream places of Iran, which is the choice of many tourists to travel in the summer season. Abundant natural, forest and mountain landscapes, Sardabroud river, greenery and freshness, extremely cool air and neighborhood with clouds and blue sky have made Klardasht known as one of the famous tourist destinations. In fact, one of its distinctive features can be considered its pleasant weather, which, unlike the northern regions of the country, does not become sharji; That is why it is known as the lost paradise.
