About Me

My name is Oguzhan . I am from Turkey and I have been in UK for almost two years. I have been in IT industry for 2 years. My main programming language is Java, which I definitely very fond of it, and I have been working as a Test Automation Engineer for almost 2 years as a volunteer. I have a strong understanding of SDLC, and I am familiar with both Agile and Waterfall methodologies. I developed Automation framework from scratch. I have successfully designed and developed many tests cases by using Data driven and behavier driven. A highly competent and results-oriented Test Automation Engineer with almost 2 years of experience in Software Testing.I am a detail-oriented, quick learner about new tools and technologies Software Engineer who has experience on manual and automation testing doing functional test, smoke test and regression test etc.
