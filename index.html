Everett Hybrid Smartwatch

The Fossil Everett is a feature-packed hybrid smartwatch with a built-in, always-on display and heart rate sensor. We like that — instead of looking like a tech product — it resembles a classic chronograph timepiece with mechanical hands and a three-button layout. The Everett can deliver notifications from your phone and keep tabs on your activities. The stainless steel timepiece is waterproof up to 30 meters. It is available in several finishes, with an easy-to-replace band or bracelet.
