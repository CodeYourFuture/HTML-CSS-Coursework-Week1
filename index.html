Fast data transfer

The Samsung T5 External SSD offers fast data transfer speeds of up to 540 MB/s, so large data such as 4K videos and high resolution photos can be transferred and backed up quickly.

Compact design

Lightweight and portable, the T5 has an all-round metal design and rounded unibody that fits comfortably in the palm of your hand. As it has no moving parts and a durable exterior, it can handle drops of up to two metres.

Management software

Embedded software for PC and Mac lets you easily setup a password and get the latest firmware updates. Alternatively you can download the app to manage the SSD on your Android device.
