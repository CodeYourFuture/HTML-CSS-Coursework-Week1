Water plays an important part in brewing a decent coffee and the quality of water can vary depending on location. A way to measure the quality of water is the "hardness". Hard water has a high number of dissolved minerals, predominantly magnesium and calcium. Water is naturally soft when it falls from the sky and gains its hardness after coming in to contact with the ground. You can check the water quality of your local area using this postcode checker.
