History of the Down Jacket

American adventurer Eddie Bauer created the world’s first down jacket in 1936, for reasons of sheer necessity rather than style. “He nearly lost his life to hypothermia whilst on a mid-winter fishing trip,” New York-based fashion curator, Shonagh Marshall, explains.
