About me

Hi! I am glad to see you here.My name is Inna Poliakova. I'm from Ukraine. I really like sports and various activities - running, hiking in the mountains, walking. In 2019, I participated in the Athens Classic Marathon and ran 42 km from the village of Marathon to the city of Athens (Greece). It was a very big challenge for me. But I love to challenge myself, set ambitious goals and achieve them. Now I face a new challenge: to get a new profession almost from scratch. Another hobby is social media. For example, I have my own YouTube channel where I share my life experience with other people.
