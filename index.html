'Drip' with the once 'infamous' Puffer

This year's top pick, here's why?

With the cold being inevitable (typical life in London) and the puffer jacket labelled the ultimate jacket to keep you warm, it's no surprise why of recent it's the top choice. This jacket has evolved into a very stylish piece of apparel and is marketed in many colors, shapes and lengths. Let's not forget that some are hooded and hoodless. Many argue that whether filled with down or synthetic fibres, the puffer jacket lives up to it's intent and does a good job at keeping us warm amid bitter temperatures.
