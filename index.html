A little about Us..

We are a volunteer led organisation helping mums in tech
achieve their full potential by career coaching,trainings,
social activities and seminars.

Services

Career Coaching

We offer coaching and support to mums who already are in the tech industry or want to get into it.
How to achieve their dream jobs or getting a promotion.

Networking

Weekly meetings with moms who are going through motherhood and the career ,
sharing thoughts and ideas and supporting one another.

Training Opportunities

We offer free training with our partner organisation in software Development,
Graphic Design ,IT Consultancy to help progress in your career.
