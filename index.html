Explore Kelardasht

The lost paradise of northern Iran

Klardasht mountain region is one of the dream places of Iran, which is the choice of many tourists to travel in the summer season. Abundant natural, forest and mountain landscapes, Sardabroud river, greenery and freshness, extremely cool air and neighborhood with clouds and blue sky have made Klardasht known as one of the famous tourist destinations. In fact, one of its distinctive features can be considered its pleasant weather, which, unlike the northern regions of the country, does not become sharji; That is why it is known as the lost paradise. Velasht Lake

Velasht freshwater lake is located in the northeast of Kalardasht and hosts many tourists every year due to its unique and enchanting landscape and unique nature. In addition to fishing, swimming and boating in the lake, you can experience other activities such as cycling and walking around the lake or watch the natural beauty of the mountains. Spring and summer season is the best time to visit Velasht Lake, and due to the availability of facilities such as cafes, restaurants, accommodation suites, campsites, restrooms, buffets, and supermarkets, you can visit this beautiful lake without any worries.
