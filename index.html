A GUIDE TO

UPCYCLING & RECYCLING PRE-LOVED ITEMS FROM YOUR CLOSET!

A SUSTAINABLE PRACTISE

By: Gayle-Ngozee - CYF Project 2022



Are there some fashion pieces in your wardrobe that you no longer wear or have gotten bored of? If so, there are now so many clever ways to recycle or upcycle your garments. Turn old pants into a dress, recycle gold and stones from one piece of jewellery into another, resell pre-loved items and even, get credit vouchers for your next purchase when you donate in some shops. Find out more below.

H&M Garment Collection Programme:

First launched in 2013, H&M's Garment Collecting programme aims to keep used clothing in circulation for longer. Simply hand in your old clothes (by any brand and in any condition), and receive a thank you voucher to use towards your next purchase. Balenciaga Re-Sell Program:

Launched in 2022, Balenciaga's Re-Sell program gives its pieces a second life, by partnering with Reflaunt to assist in the re-selling of pre-loved Balenciaga pieces, enabling you to become part of the circular fashion revolution. Currently available in the US, UK, Italy, France and Singapore, you can book a collection or in-store drop-off for your items.
