About Me

I am Vanipriya Piramanathan, living in Coventry, UK. I was born and brought up in India. I did my schooling in my hometown, Nattuchalai, a small village near Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu. I got so much interest in Mathematics which enabled me to score full marks in my board exams 10th and 12th standard. I have completed my Bachelor's of Engineering in Electronics from Alagappa Chettiyar Engineering College, Karaikudi. After my Bachelor's, I joined Masters' in communication systems at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Chennai. During my college days, I involved myself in coding activities and stopped it since I changed my domain. Now, I want to improve my coding skills, so I joined code your future.
