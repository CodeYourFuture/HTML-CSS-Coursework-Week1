VBC's Short Story
The founder is based within the UK and is a MSc & BA (Hons) Graduate.
VBC began in her bedroom making nail polish 6 years ago,
discovering the polish ingredients were accidentally vegan.
She realised there was a gap in the market.
VBC specialises in hand making artisan indie polish -
nail polish that is 10-free, cruelty-free and vegan friendly.
Fast forward to 2020, the business has expanded with more
items such as, customised novelty bath-bombs and other bespoke cosmetics.
